About

Based in Brisbane,  King Velvet is an emerging Funk/Soul artist who takes influence from artists like Durand Jones & The Indications, Anderson .Paak, and Leon Bridges.

With a mixture of raw, bluesy vocals, live instrumentation, soulful chord progressions, and a production style that leans heavily in the direction of hip-hop, King Velvet’s aesthetic is both unique, yet harkens back to sounds and subject matter which are familiar and timeless.

King Velvet’s first release ‘Wake Up’ is one which explores themes of love, lust, and kinship, and it does so over a musical backdrop which features some of Brisbane’s finest musicians and songwriters.

‘Wake Up’ is available on all streaming platforms now.
